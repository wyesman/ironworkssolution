## Page Structure

Each service page includes:

### 1. Hero Section
- Service name as H1
- Compelling value proposition
- Two CTAs (Schedule Consultation + Contact)
- Service icon

### 2. Introduction Section
- 3 detailed paragraphs explaining the service
- Benefits and value proposition
- Target audience identification
- Expertise and experience highlights

### 3. Benefits Grid
- 4 key benefits specific to the service
- Icon-based design
- Clear value statements

### 4. Services Grid
- 5-6 specific sub-services
- Image for each sub-service
- 4 key features per sub-service
- Hover effects and animations

### 5. Additional Content Section
- Process steps, industries served, or popular applications
- Varies by service to provide unique value

### 6. Call-to-Action Section
- Strong headline
- Persuasive copy
- Two CTAs
- Trust indicators (stats/credentials)

All URLs follow the pattern: `/services/{service-name-slug}`
- Lowercase
- Hyphen-separated
- Descriptive keywords

- **Length:** 1,500-2,500 words per page
